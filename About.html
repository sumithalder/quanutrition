What Happens During The Nurtrition Consultation?

The initial consultation will usually last between 60 and 90 minutes, and your Nutritionist will try to gather as much information relevant from you. Indepth probing will happen to understand your profile and body behaviour. From understanding and analysing this, Best Nutritionist will aim to identify nutritional, diet and lifestyle factors that could be contributing to and/or underlying the symptoms and health conditions that you are experiencing.

You will then come back for a personalised, safe, lifestyle Nutrition Diet Plan with consultation that is evidence based, and tailored towards your very individual requirements. We may recommended supplements, super foods and/or functional tests if your Nutritionist feels could be beneficial in order to better assess if you are deficient in any vitamins and minerals or allergy and heavy metal tests.

A follow up consultation is generally recommended for 4-5 weeks later, Nutritionists will also provide some level of telephone and/or e-mail support between sessions to monitor progress. Further follow-ups may be required depending on your individual situation and program that you have chosen normally once in a week connect.

One of the top questions we are asked “What exactly do you do?” It’s a great question, because what a nutritionist does is not as straightforward. Our best answer is this – we help you figure out where you are, where you want to be, and how to get there in terms of your health and fitness all by tweaking scientifically the way you eat !

  • Many people are worried that we are going to scold them, lecture them, or take away their favourite foods. There are some nutritionists like that, but we do not believe that philosophy. We consider ourselves more of a food coach, because our goal is to inform, inspire, counsel, and support you, and we want to see you succeed!
  • As we work with you as a personal nutrition trainer, our style is much more about helping you understand your bodies and fall in love with being healthy.
  • First we understand what you are looking for to get a perspective on how we can map our progress together.
  • We then do a thorough nutrition assessment, which includes information about your weight history, current and past medical history, family medical history, food allergies or intolerances, likes and dislikes, eating, sleeping and exercise habits, emotional and social ties to food and much more.
  • Then we develop a game plan together for how to proceed.
  • Some people prefer a formal, structured eating plan. Others do much better with a short list of changes that are specific and measurable. We explain the reasoning behind the plan or changes, including exactly how they will affect your body and what you can expect.
  • After the guidance is given, we ask most of my clients to communicate with us every week, either via email or phone.
  • We endeavour to connect back to review the improvements on a monthly basis, thereby taken time and essentially realigning the Nutrition guidance to go forward as you move forward in your goals. Fluidity in the Nutrition plan is as important as the change that we look to make for ourselves and is a reflection of the same. We ask you to be patient as every food plan takes 8 to 20 weeks to show positive results.
