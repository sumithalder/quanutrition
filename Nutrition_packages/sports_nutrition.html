Hair

Our hair health is a barometer of our nutrition falsies. Some people are blessed with great hair genetically and others choose to loose it over a lifetime due to neglect and ignorance.

Your diet is extremely important to the health of your hair, and its impacton your hairsappearance and growth should not be underestimated. Your hair is comprised of the second fastest-growing cells in your body – intestinal cells being the first. Your scalp has roughly 120,000 hairs growing at any given time, all of which need to be supported. However, the most challenging part of sustaining hair growth is this: the body will never prioritize its nutrition needs as hair is not a vital organ or tissue. Due to its expendable nature, any nutritional imbalance will often show up first in the form of excessive hair loss.

Hair and scalp problems can arise from either a deficiency or an excess of nutrients in your diet, and in some cases nutrition alone can be the cause of hair loss.

However, many people have been led to believe is a ‘healthy diet’ most often does not include all of the necessary elements needed for optimum hair growth, health and appearance.

At Qua Nutrition, a team of expert nutritionists will design a nutrition plan addressing this concern of yours and soon you look towards the shoots regrowth as your crowning glory.
