Why Qua?

QUA Nutrition, we realize that each person is different, with varied health and sports_nutrition needs. Our motto ‘Eat to your capacity’ caters to each individual’s specific health requirement. Thus, discovering “your capacity” synonymous to Qua!

THE BEST NUTRITION CLINIC

The Qua Nutrition experience is one in which you meet a world class best Nutritionist, available to you world wide ( via online portal, skype telephonic, across time zones), in the speciality you want, working with you to achieve your goals with a Customised Nutrition Plan on the most advanced Nutrition planning software in the world.

Best Nutrition Programs

At Qua Nutrition, we believe in understanding what you are looking for and gradually modify your diet plans to bringing permanent changes in your life for better health. Changing nutrition behaviour based on your comfort level, age, genes, goals, time lines and other factors determine the success of the Nutrition plan.
